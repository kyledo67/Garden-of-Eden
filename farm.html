Our Sustainable Farm

01

Seed Selection & Planning

We carefully select organic, heirloom seeds and plan our crop rotation to maintain soil health. Our seasonal planning ensures we grow the perfect vegetables for each season while preserving biodiversity.

02

Sustainable Growing Practices

Our farm uses natural composting, rainwater harvesting, and companion planting techniques. We maintain soil health through organic methods and protect beneficial insects that help our garden thrive.

03

Mindful Harvesting

We harvest our vegetables at peak ripeness, using traditional hand-picking methods to ensure the highest quality. Each vegetable is carefully inspected and handled with care to maintain its nutritional value.

04

Farm to Table Journey

Our harvested vegetables go directly to our restaurant kitchen, where our chefs transform them into delicious vegetarian dishes. This direct farm-to-table approach ensures the freshest, most flavorful dining experience.
