How does Farm-to-Table impact Farmers?

Farm-to-table supports local farmers by providing them with direct sales opportunities, reducing their reliance on large distributors, and helping them earn fairer prices for their produce. It also encourages sustainable farming practices and strengthens local food economies.

Did You Know?
